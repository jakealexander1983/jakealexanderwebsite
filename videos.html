Videos

The outbreak of Covid-19 in early 2020 forced musicians to come up with new ways of communicating. Here is a collection of videos I created during Lockdown that reflects my effort to keep things going! It includes snippets from online rehearsals, virtual choir projects I have led, often in collaboration with other artists, musical directors and choirs, and one-off performances I've filmed to inspire my singers and help them to learn songs remotely.
